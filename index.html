About me

Estonian // 21

Hey there, my name is Rei and I’m a Multimedia Design student from KEA - Københavns Erhvervsakademi. I’m originally from Estonia and I have chosen to make Copenhagen my new home to pursue my passion to become a web-designer.
I have chosen the Front-End Development elective as my 3rd semester subject as I feel that expanding my skillset with hard skills such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript and various frameworks such as React, MotionOne and Sass along with the Adobe Suite and Figma is the best way to make myself valuable in the digital design world.
The projects listed above are a very small selection of work I have done so far during my ongoing studies at KEA.
